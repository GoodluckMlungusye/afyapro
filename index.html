AfyaPro 2.0 connected care

AeHS is a pan-African network that focusses on developing high-quality, affordable ICT-based solutions which enable health care providers in Africa to further improve the quality of their health services. The first priority is to deliver the best care to the clients or patients. At the same time, health care providers have to deal with many day-to-day challenges ranging from managing patient waiting times and collating and safeguarding patient data to revenue collection and managing medical supplies.

Intelligent Hospital Solution

Health care facilities operate in a complex environment. The first priority is to deliver the best care to the clients or patients.

AfyaPro enables health facilities and clinics to take full control of their administration to enhance performance and increase revenue. AfyaPro not only provides easy access to patientsâ€™ medical records it also allows healthcare providers to continuously keep track of patient information, In addition, AfyaPro supports billing and accounting and helps staff to manage HR, assets, and stocks (pharmacy). By keeping track of this information, health care facilities and clinics save money and improve data quality and accuracy, which in turn results in more effective and efficient health care delivery.

Patient Mobile App

The use of a mobile App and call centre technology is a smart technology that has proven to be very useful in the African context.

The mobile App and the call centre dashboard are seamlessly integrated in the hospital HMIS-EMR. Whether the patient is actually visiting the clinic or just using your services remotely all is integrated in one AfyaPro solution.Our package includes remote management of chronic diseases and everything for a modern health facility. This makes it very attractive for managing chronic conditions such as diabetes and hypertension. For a doctor this a best way solution and an affordable approach to manage large patient numbers. Patient adherence, clinical results and medical reputation all improve.

Benefits to the clients

AfyaPro makes the difference in your health facility

AfyaPro for Management & Administrative Organisation

Our solution reduces the operational costs and gives you the ability to track service and work performance through actionable weekly and monthly reports. AfyaPro registers all payments and transactions, resulting in transparency and a significant increase of facility revenue. AfyaPro is linked to the public health reporting (DHIS2). Different health facilities can be connected. Management can follow the activities at different sites. Patients can be transferred without the headache of sending a paper folder over to the other site.

AfyaPro for Medical Professionals

Our solution allows you for the same standards of support as European and American doctors. AfyaPro empowers you to do what you want to do: provide the best services, a minimum of time spent behind the computer, building a strong reputation, and making your work efficient. AfyaPro allows teamwork and telemedicine with other specialists who remotely log in.

AfyaPro for Patients

AfyaPro brings quality care to you as a patient and gives insight in your personal care path. It reduces waiting times, gives access to your medical file via the patient portal and enables you to schedule appointments.
