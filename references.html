In the meantime you deal with challenges in the field of patient waiting time, secure and private patient data collection, revenue collection and to manage the medical supplies. Nyakahanga Hospital

Hospital located at Nyakahanga

A basic hospital information system that follows the patient’s journey: registration, vital signs, consultation, lab, pharmacy, In patient department (nursing Station). It functions over four years. The initial investment was partially sponsored. Now it’s completely running based on own resources. The advantage of AfyaPro was that it created transparency and substantially increased the hospital revenue. As a consequence additional means are available for expansion of medical services.
